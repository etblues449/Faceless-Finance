What it does

The desk that doesn't remember you.

Client-side portfolio engine

Every position, P&L, and risk metric is computed in your browser. The server sees encrypted blobs, never balances.

PORTFOLIO · 30D +18.4%

Passkey sign-in

No email. No phone. A passkey on your device proves who you are without telling us who you are.

Non-custodial

Keys never leave your hardware. We couldn't freeze your funds even if a court asked.

Sub-second routing

Orders fan out across 31 relays in parallel — the fastest fill wins. Median execution lands at 11.4ms, P99 at 38ms.

Onion-routed orderflow

Every leg of an order hops through three relays before reaching a venue. No node ever sees both your identity and your trade.

Pricing

Free during beta.
Self-host forever after.

We don't charge you to keep your own money. When we leave beta, you'll be able to pay $14/month for hosted relays — or run the daemon yourself for nothing.
